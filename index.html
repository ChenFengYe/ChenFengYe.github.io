About Me

Hi, this is Xin Chen(陈欣). I am currently 1st year PhD student in Virtual Reality and Visual Computing (VRVC) center, School of Information Science and Technology(SIST), ShanghaiTech University, advised by Prof. Jingyi Yu. Before this, I was supervised by Dr. Youyi Zheng for two years studying on computer graphics .

My research focuses on in computer vision and graphics, especially human performance capture, image-based modeling, deep learning and so on.
